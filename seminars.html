MOOSE (Moduli of Other Spaces Etc.) Seminar

In Spring 2015, Patricio Gallardo and Anna Kazanova organized a seminar within the algebraic geometry group at UGA. We have investigated questions concerning moduli spaces of trees of projective spaces with marked points. These spaces were first defined by Chen, Gibney, and Krashen (arXiv). Have a look at the seminar website here.

Student Algebraic Geometry Seminar
